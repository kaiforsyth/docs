## Syncing Discord channels

[Discord forum channels](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/6208479917079-Forum-Channels-FAQ)

[Discord text channels](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/4412085582359-Text-Channels-Text-Chat-In-Voice-Channels)

To sync channels from Discord, navigate to [**Channels** in the admin dashboard](https://dashboard.usehall.com/channels), then select:
1. **Manage** for Discord.
2. Go to the **Synced Discord channels** section.
3. Click **Add a channel**.
4. Select the channels you wish to sync.
